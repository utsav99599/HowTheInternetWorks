Google

Google was building the internet search platform that lead them success of the internet search company after 2 to 3 years.

    • Google CO founders Larry Page and Sergey Brin
    • Larry Page and Sergey Brin were looking into an internet search solution
    • In 1998 Google came up with internet search by counting links that important.
    • Google and Excite company meet up to bring new ideas about internet search.
    • Excite company was not very excited about internet search because they did not see the future. Also, Excite company did not know how google will generate income.
    • Excite was trying to buy google for million dollars but Google co-founders refused to take that offer.
    • Larry page went to talk with Andrew E. Rubin and demo the google page. Andy saw the future of internet search and he wrote a check to google $100,000.00 as an investment.
    • Slowly investor is increasing, and Google started organizing internet search by different categories such as education, car, financial, etc.
    • There was still a problem with how to make money from internet searches because Google needs to buy the storing equipment for the company such as the server room, pay to programmers, etc.
    • The user is looking up something on google it will track the keywords that help users to get information.
    • Bill gross solve the google ideas about making money from an internet search. Bill gross owned Idealab company, he had several meetings with Google co-founders. Bill gross explained about yellow book strategies. When the user types something in the internet search pull up the keyword that keyword will link to a different page that can help the user to find the information.
    • Google co-founder left the meeting without saying anything to bill gross and copy bill gross idea and build the internet search by google algorithm with an organized link page.
    • Google company shows the keywords data to the advertisement companies. Then Google started generating income from Google.
