Important People

YouTube

Chad Hurley founder of the you-Tube in February 2005. He wants to connect people by sharing media. YouTube created hope in normal people's life by showing the talent to the world by posting on YouTube. In 2006 Google Bought the You-Tube company.

Digg

Jay Adelson Digg Co-founder of Dig company in November 2004. Digg is a user blog system user post the latest information about news and post on the Digg site. Then users read the news from Digg sites.

MySpace
Chris Dewolfe founder of my space company in 2005. There were 27 million users were register and connect with people, chatting with long-distance, listening to the free music that was access to music files.

Facebook

Mark Zuckerberg Co-founder of Facebook in February 2004. He started Facebook in the campus and recommend their friend to open the account. Facebook grew 10 million active users and double every 6 months. Facebook is about connecting people by the social graph.

Napster
Shawn Fanning founder of Napster in June 1999. Napster is about sharing music files from one to another by PC. People can go to the Napster site and search for music then download. June 1999 Shawn wrote the code and share with his friends and told them to not share the Napster; However, Napster got sperate in a week most of the people got access to download Napster app and download free music store in the pc to mp3 player. This music was downloaded for free that causing artists to not get paid for their music. July 2001 Napster close the company by a lawsuit by the music industry.
