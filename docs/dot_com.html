Dot Com Bubble

Amazon

Jeff Bezos created an amazon company for the book. He discovers that there are millions of books around the world, it was hard to store them in the book shell. He started a company renting and selling books online. Amazon was getting a hard time on the online payment system. Online payment was not successful in 1996 due to the credit card transaction. Later the year amazon solved the problem of credit card transaction. May 1997 IPO came for amazon's online book business successful. Today Amazon is the most successful company because the business model was to create the win customer trust. Providing the customer wants for daily use and getting product faster and cheaper.

eBay

Pierre Morad and jeff have one thing in common the internet is for business. eBay planned to create a marketplace to sell the thing to people and bring people in one place. It is bid and options products. List the items on the eBay site and whoever has a higher bid takes the products. He created the whole eBay site by himself on the holiday weekend in September 1995. Today eBay still exists because eBay created the best place for bid price on the item that most valuable.

Bubble Pop

Amazon and eBay change the economy by increasing the Nasdaq index. Computer science and programmer were creating e-commerce companies to get quick millions. It was a new economy for e-commerce. In 1995 there was no more than 30 internet IPO and by end of 1999, there was over 250 Internet IPO. People were buying stocks of the new e-commerce companies. Brokers were selling tech companies and the broker did not even know if the companies will last longer. From 1995 to 2000 internet bubble build up super high in the stock market and Nasdaq drop mover than 350 points in history.
